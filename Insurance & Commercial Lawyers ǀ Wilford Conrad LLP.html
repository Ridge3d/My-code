About Our Firm

Wilford Conrad LLP is a boutique law firm dedicated to the representation of international and domestic insurance companies and business entities of all sizes. The firm specializes in insurance coverage analysis, advice and litigation, the direct defense of insured entities and individuals in all manner of litigation, and the representation of businesses and individuals in commercial disputes. From its office in Barrington, Illinois – conveniently located close to the United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois and the Circuit Courts of Cook, Lake, and McHenry Counties – the firm represents clients in various state and federal courts in Illinois as well as numerous state and federal courts across the country.
